Movement Over Pain

Are you are deailing with some kind of difficult painful problem?

If so, you have probably been told to exercise, but you may have found that exercising in a way that doesn't make you hurt more can be challenging.

I am a physical therapist and many of my clients have either had bad experiences with exercise or haven't even tried for fear of hurting more.

A very common issue is applying a fitness mindset to a painful problem. However, I have found that applying pain science principles to the exercise process has helped many finally improve their fitness without hurting more.

If you are curious what a pain science approach to exercise looks like, join Movement Over Pain to get an email mini-course on the basic concepts. It's free and it just might give you some ideas to reintroduce healthy movement into your life.
